ADVERTISEMENTS

Many websites and free apps often contain advertisements. Advertisements themselves aren't necessarily a dark pattern, it's okay to include them in your website or free app as long as they are not intrusive.

They only become a dark pattern if they become intrusive to the user experience, how advertising may become intrusive can be through the following methods:

  • Interrupting the intended experience by forcing users to watch an ad.
    • This ad could be an unskippable ad, meaning users have to go the full length watching the ad.
  • Tricking you into clicking on an ad by placing it where you will often click on something.
